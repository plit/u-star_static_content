The company

Creighton Strategies Ltd., a Gibraltar based company, is the holding company of the worldwide rights of U-STAR format. RisingStars Asia Online Ltd. PTE is a Singapore based media company, established in 2011, holding licensing rights to U-STAR in ASEAN. U-STAR was organised first a series of online singing contests through its internet karaoke and competition platform. The next step was adding its unique broadcast-quality mobile application recording, and competition features, and the development of the first TV show format fully integrated with smart phone interactivity. The first season aired in the Philippines under the name of RisingStars Philippines on the TV5 Network, Saturdays at 10 pm, starting in mid March 2015, and running for 10 weeks. The programme was hosted by celebrity Ogie Alcasid, with Miss Universe runner up Venus Raj as his partner and was a hit with the young generations as one of the top trending TV shows in twitter and Facebook
