From phone to fame - the RisingStars TV Show


Most TV talent shows are the same, right? They queue, audition, and then sing in front of a group of celebrity judges. Well, not anymore! RisingStars Asia has just aired the first season of its revolutionary talent show format in the Philippines, merging the concept of a traditional TV show with online and mobile technologies. With online auditions and integrated, real-time audience interaction, the smartphone now becomes a key element of the programme, bringing unprecedented excitement and unpredictability into the mix!
